# Harbor — Partnership Deck Outline
## For organizations like AARP, Area Agencies on Aging, Medicare Advantage plans, health systems

---

### Slide 1: The Problem
- 53 million Americans are caregivers for aging family members
- Most are unprepared: 60%+ have no legal documents, no medication list, no care plan
- When a crisis hits (fall, hospitalization, diagnosis), families scramble — wrong decisions get made, critical information is missing, and caregivers burn out
- The cost of unpreparedness: longer hospital stays, unsafe discharges, preventable re-admissions, caregiver health decline

### Slide 2: Who We Serve
- Adult children (typically 45-65) managing care for an aging parent
- The "sandwich generation" — balancing their own lives, careers, and children with a parent's growing needs
- They are not healthcare professionals — they need guidance, not just information
- Your members / your patients' families / your employees

### Slide 3: What Harbor Does
- An AI-powered caregiving readiness and navigation platform
- Two entry points: proactive readiness assessment OR real-time crisis support
- Helps families organize critical information, execute on legal and medical paperwork, and stay ahead of their parent's changing needs
- Not a static checklist — an ongoing care platform with AI monitoring, weekly briefings, and adaptive task management

### Slide 4: The Readiness Path (Proactive)
- Guided assessment across 6 domains: medical, legal, financial, housing, transportation, social
- Readiness score (0-100) with critical gap identification
- Prioritized action plan with execution support
- State-specific guided legal forms: Power of Attorney, Advance Directives, HIPAA Authorization — with the actual forms, witness/notary requirements, step-by-step checklists
- Goal: get families organized before a crisis forces it

### Slide 5: The Crisis Path (Reactive)
- AI-powered real-time triage when something goes wrong
- Auto-generates prioritized tasks from the conversation
- Crisis playbooks for common emergencies (falls, stroke, cognitive decline, hospitalization)
- ER triage sheet, printable wallet card, exportable care summaries
- Goal: structured decision support in the most stressful moments

### Slide 6: Ongoing AI Care Platform
- Background monitoring agents watch for care-relevant signals: medication risks, policy changes, deadlines, health concerns
- Judgment agent scores each signal against the parent's specific profile
- Weekly AI briefings surface what matters, ranked by urgency
- Life event reporting triggers task cascades (fall → home safety + doctor follow-up + housing reassessment)
- The platform adapts as the parent's situation evolves

### Slide 7: Key Capabilities
- Document intelligence: upload a photo of an insurance card or med list → auto-extracted structured data
- Facility search: CMS Medicare quality data, filterable by distance and rating
- Medication tracking with refill urgency
- Appointment prep with AI-generated doctor questions
- Data export for ER visits, attorney meetings, family discussions
- Calendar-aware nudges (Medicare enrollment, wellness visits, refill reminders)

### Slide 8: Why Partner with Harbor

#### For AARP / Member Organizations:
- Tangible member benefit that addresses the #1 concern of 45-65 demographic
- Increases member engagement and perceived value of membership
- Positions organization as the trusted resource for caregiving — not just information, but action
- White-label or co-branded options available

#### For Medicare Advantage Plans:
- Reduces preventable ER visits and hospital readmissions
- Improves care transition outcomes (discharge readiness, medication reconciliation)
- Supports HEDIS/Stars measures related to care coordination
- Caregiver engagement correlates with better patient outcomes

#### For Health Systems:
- Improves discharge outcomes — families arrive prepared, leave with a plan
- Reduces readmission penalties (30-day readmission reduction)
- Extends care coordination beyond hospital walls
- Complements existing care navigation programs

#### For Employers:
- Elder caregiving costs employers $34B/year in lost productivity
- Reduces absenteeism and presenteeism from caregiving stress
- High-value, differentiated employee benefit
- Caregiver wellness monitoring helps prevent burnout-related leave

### Slide 9: Partnership Models
- **Distribution**: Harbor promoted to members/patients/employees as a recommended tool or included benefit
- **Co-branded**: Partner-branded version of Harbor integrated into existing member/patient portal
- **Clinical integration**: Harbor as the family-facing layer of existing care coordination programs
- **Data insights** (anonymized, aggregated): caregiving readiness benchmarks, gap analysis by region/demographic — valuable for program design and policy

### Slide 10: Traction & Roadmap
- MVP live with readiness assessment, crisis intake, guided legal forms, AI monitoring/briefings, facility search, document intelligence
- Roadmap: hospital companion mode, medication safety layer, cognitive decline tracking, caregiver wellness, Medicare/Medicaid enrollment guides, family sharing
- Built on: Next.js, Anthropic Claude AI, Supabase, PostgreSQL
